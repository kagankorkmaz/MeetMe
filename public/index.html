About Us

We are a group of university students enrolled in Sabanci University. All of out team attend meetings all the time, some for school clubs, some for internship interviews, some for project meetings. On top of a busy school schedule, finding and arranging a time for each meeting has proven to be an extremely difficult for us and definately is aburden on top of other resposibilities piling up, thus the project Meet Me was born. We wanted a solution to this problem, and Meet Me is out answer!
